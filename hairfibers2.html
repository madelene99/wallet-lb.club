Subgenus of Hair fibers
Solution gives the instant appearance of a thick, full head of hair. Hair Building Fibers are made from natural Keratin of the highest grade, the same type of protein found in real hair. The Fibers have a strong natural static charge that creates a magnetic effect, tightly binding Hair Building Fibers to even the finest strands of human hair. Answers about hair fibers
Question:
WHAT ARE HAIR BUILDING FIBERS MADE OF ?
Answer:
Hair Building Fibers are made of Keratin protein - the same thing that makes up human hair. The Keratin protein comes from a natural wool source, which is the closest match to human hair.
Question:
IF IT BEGINS TO RAIN, WILL HAIR BUILDING FIBERS REMAIN IN MY HAIR?
Answer:
Hair Building Fibers stay firmly in place even in wind, rain and perspiration. For even longer-lasting results and added shine, use any hair spray
Question:
DO HAIR BUILDING FIBERS AFFECT HAIR GROWTH?
Answer:
Hair Building Fibers do not encourage or discourage hair growth. The natural Keratin Fibers will not clog pores or affect a scalp condition.
Question:
HOW LONG WILL HAIR BUILDING FIBERS STAY IN PLACE?
Answer:
Hair Building Fibers stay until you wash your hair. They may shift but can be easily touched up. Many individuals who go to the salon weekly for a shampoo/set or a blow out will leave the Fibers in until their next appointment.
Question:
CAN I USE HAIR BUILDING FIBERS WITH STYLING TOOLS AND PRODUCTS?
Answer:
It is recommended that Hair Building Fibers be applied after the use of any styling tools such as hairdryers, flat irons or curling irons. Styling products (other than hairspray) should be applied to the hair prior to applying Hair Building Fibers. Applying gels, mousses, pastes, serums, and the like after applying Fibers may cause shifting the Fibers and could expose the areas that were meant to be covered. Let styling products dry before applying Fibers.
Question.:
DO HAIR BUILDING FIBERS WORK ON ETHNIC HAIR?
Answer:
The texture and appearance of the Hair Building Fibers work well with ethnic hair.
Question.:
HOW LONG WILL A BOTTLE OF HAIR BUILDING FIBERS LAST?
Answer:
It depends on how frequently you use the product, the amount of the product you use each time. But in general 1 bottle will last for 15-20 usages
