How Instantly Ageless Works
The main active ingredient, hexapeptide Argireline, which is no less qualitative than botulinum toxin, smooths wrinkles, however, does not harm the skin.
Upon entering the basal layer of the epidermis, the component acts on the motor nerves, causing the alignment of the facial musculature, its increase and the adoption of a physiological position, while eliminating muscular spasm and straightening the wrinkles.
The main reason for the appearance of mimic wrinkles is muscle spasm, which causes shortening of the facial muscles and the formation of wrinkles from excess skin. Over time, muscles lose their volume, accumulation and stagnation of the lymphatic fluid occurs, edema is formed.
Cream Instantly Ageless causes relaxation of the facial muscles, slowing the conductivity of nerve impulses, but not blocking their intercellular transmission and not disrupting the mobility of the facial expression of a woman.
